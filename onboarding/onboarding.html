Step 2 of 4

What TabFiler can do

Two permissions, both used only on your machine. Nothing is ever sent anywhere.

  • ⊞
    Read your open tabsSo it can see each page's title and decide where it belongs.
  • ★
    Manage your bookmarksSo it can create folders and save pages into them.

Step 3 of 4

How sorting works

Keywords in the title decide the folder. When several match, the category higher in this list wins — so an Android security page goes to Android, not Security.

  1. 1Android
  2. 2iOS
  3. 3Kubernetes / Docker
  4. 4Security
  5. 5Mobile
  6. 6Unknown

You can rename folders, change keywords, and reorder priority any time in Settings.
